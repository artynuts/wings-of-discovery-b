Who made this site?

Wings Of Discovery is a simple website made by kids who are curious about drones and artificial intelligence. It's a place to share projects, ideas, and things we learn.

Our goals

  • Learn how drones can help people understand places.
  • Explore how computers can learn from pictures.
  • Share fun, safe projects that other kids can try with adults.

Contact

If you are an adult who wants to help or learn more, open the project files and get in touch with the teams who made this site (contact info is kept private).
