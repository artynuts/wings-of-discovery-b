What is drone mapping?

Drone mapping means using a small flying drone to take lots of pictures of a place and then turning those pictures into a map or 3D view. It helps people see places from above.

How we learn about it

  • Plan a safe flight area (parks or open spaces).
  • Fly the drone slowly and take many pictures.
  • Use software (on a computer) to join the pictures into a map.

Fun ideas

Compare two maps from different days, look for changes, or make a colourful map to show to friends.

Note: Always follow local rules and fly with a grown-up.
