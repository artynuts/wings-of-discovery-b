What is AI image detecting?

AI image detecting means teaching a computer to look at a picture and find things. For example, it can find trees, cars, or animals in pictures.

Simple way to explore

  1. Collect pictures (with permission) of things you want to find.
  2. Label the pictures (draw boxes or write what is in each picture).
  3. Use kid-friendly tools or a teacher's help to train a model that learns from the labels.

Kid projects
